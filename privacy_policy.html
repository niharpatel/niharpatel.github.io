Privacy Policy

We do our best to maintain strict privacy and confidentiality. We do not track users visits to our site nor log any form inputs they make that could be medical information. We do, however, provide methods for individuals or organizations to contact us. These cases will generate a contact through a third-party service are are not logged or stored on our servers but may be logged on third party systems.

Data Policy

Our dataset is anonymized data with PII eliminated from it. However, due to the sensitive nature of this data in general and the right of individuals participating in research studies to have their information protected, we will not distribute our core dataset due to ethical concerns. This can be accessed directly through the National Alzhiemer Coordinating Center by request. However, we consider our model open sourced as they contain no private information and reconstruction of individual data from the model would be impossible.
